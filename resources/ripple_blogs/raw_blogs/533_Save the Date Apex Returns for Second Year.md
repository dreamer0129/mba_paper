Team Ripple

Building on the success of the inaugural XRP Ledger developer summit, the XRP Ledger Foundation and Ripple are proud to announce that Apex is back for its second year!

The event—taking place September 6-8, 2022—brings together developers, contributors and thought leaders in crypto to learn, build, share, network and celebrate all things XRP Ledger (XRPL)—ultimately inspiring new use cases and projects.

Why Attend?

Apex showcases the many applications—as well as the latest features and developments—being built on the open-source XRP Ledger, by its global developer community. Attendees can expect live breakout sessions, workshops and demos featuring the ideas and projects coming to life on the XRP Ledger.

More than 800 developers and XRPL community members convened in person and virtually for last year’s event, which featured more than 30 sessions from 35 speakers across Las Vegas and Tallin, Estonia.

Developers of all levels—from beginner to expert—are encouraged to attend Apex 2022 as a way to meet, connect and collaborate with the XRPL community. Registration to attend will open in the coming weeks, so stay tuned to make sure you don’t miss the XRPL developer summit and coming speaker announcements!
