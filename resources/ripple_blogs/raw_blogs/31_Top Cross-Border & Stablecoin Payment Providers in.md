Stablecoins are digital assets minted and stored on the blockchain that are designed to maintain a stable value by pegging its worth to a reserve asset. They are most commonly fiat-backed stablecoins in which the value is pegged to a fiat currency like the US dollar or euro. Because of this, they can offer greater price stability than other types of cryptocurrencies, making them an ideal entry point into the blockchain ecosystem for more risk-averse financial institutions.
